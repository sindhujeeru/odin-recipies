Chicken Tikka

Description

Chicken Tikka Kebabs in a richly spiced yogurt marinade. Grilled, barbecued or griddled to perfection, they make an easy, family-pleasing dinner! Serve them simply with rice or chapati, or go all out with a big Indian buffet

Ingredients

  • Natural yogurt
  • Garlic, ginger and lemon juice
  • Chopped chicken breast meat
  • vegetable oil
  • Spices - ground coriander, turmeric, cumin, paprika, mild chilli powder, cinnamon, salt, black pepper
  • coriander leaves

Steps to Prepare

  1. Place the chicken pieces into a bowl with yogurt, garlic, ginger, lemon juice, coriander, turmeric, cumin, paprika, chilli powder, cinnamon, salt and pepper. Mix together, cover and marinate in the fridge for 2-3 hours.
  2. Thread onto skewers, then brush with oil.
  3. Cook on a griddle plate, under the grill (broiler) or on the barbecue for 8-10 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Serve with a sprinkling of freshly chopped coriander
